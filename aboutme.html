I am a Research Software Engineer at the Centre for Advanced Research Computing, University College London and have been since June 2023. I work across a range of projects from improving the performance High Performance Fortran to implementing existing workflows in sustainable python applications. Check out my GitHub to see what I've been up to lately.

I graduated from the University of York in 2020 with a First Class Masters Degree in Physics. My master's dissertation focused on 1D modelling of turbulence in Tokamak fusion reactors.

In my spare time I enjoy playing underwater hockey. I began playing at university before graduating to the city team, York Octopush. I now train with Islington Underwater Hockey.

I love hiking around Britain. In particular, I have been trying to walk all the Wainwright fells.
